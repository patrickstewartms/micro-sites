Trip for two to London, England – Valued at $7,950

Enjoy two premium economy round trip tickets from Toronto to London on Air Canada and a four night stay. You’ll receive two nights at the anthenaeum, a small family owned 5 star hotel in Mayfair, London, with big personality. You’ll also receive two nights at the Savoy, a Fairmont managed five star hotel, one of the most celebrated of London landmarks. Very close to the anthenaeum, the Savoy is steps away from the ballet, opera and theatre of vibrant Covent Garden. London is a diverse and exciting city with some of the world’s best sights, attractions and activities. See the city from above on the London Eye; meet a celebrity at Madame Tussauds; examine the world’s most precious treasures at the British Museum, the possibilities are endless.

Trip for two to Barbados – Valued at $5,200

The irresistible beauty of Barbados is well rooted in its legendary beaches and the warmth and hospitality of its people. The richness of the local culture and its vibrant scenery, together with a fantastic climate, have created an alluring place to escape and live amidst an equally captivating landscape. Enjoy two round trip tickets on WestJet, two nights at the Fairmont Hotel in Barbados and two nights at the Hilton Barbados. The Fairmont Royal Pavilion is one of the most exclusive luxury resorts in Barbados, situated 29km from the airport and 14km from the capital city of Bridgetown, and half a mile from the nearest shopping centre in Holetown. The Hilton Barbados is set on a gorgeous peninsula with two white sand beaches and is a dream come true for travelers in search of adventure and stunning natural beauty.

Trip for two to Montreal – Valued at $1,600

Enjoy two round trip tickets to Montreal on Via Rail and a two night stay at Le Crystal, a 5 star hotel in the heart of Montreal. You’ll enjoy personalized service and staff members who are experts at anticipating your needs without intruding on your privacy. Hotel amenities include an indoor saltwater pool as well as an outdoor hot tub and Swedish Sauna. Enjoy panoramic views of the Montreal skyline and treat yourself to a night out at Le Crystal’s Bar & Lounge.

Trip for two to Montreal – Valued at $1,800

If the train isn’t your thing, bid on this second trip for two to Montreal that includes two round trip tickets on Porter Airlines and a two night stay at the W Montreal. The W Montreal can be found by the Centre de Commerce mondial in Downtown’s Quartier international. This hip hotel in the historic Banque du Canada building is a 3 minute walk from the Square-Victoria-OACI metro station and a 7 minute walk from the Notre-Dame Basilica. It boasts a number of amenities including a spa, a trendy modern seafood restaurant and three lively bars with DJ nights.
